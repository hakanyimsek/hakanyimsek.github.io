Vestel Akıllı Mağaza

Vestel Akıllı Mağaza is my graduation project of METU Department of Industrial Design. It designed in collabration with Vestel UX/UI Department. The project aims to use the advantages of both traditional and online shopping to encourage customers to buy. It consists of three applications that are runned on different type of devices and an express store with small space.
